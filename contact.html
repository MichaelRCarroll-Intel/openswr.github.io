OpenSWR Contact

To contact us, please send us an email at openswr@googlegroups.com.

If you have problems running Mesa with OpenSWR, please use the Mesa issue tracker on GitLab. Be sure to add swr label to your issue.

Please note that OpenSWR primarily targets CPU-based rasterization for visualization purposes, not for games. We suggest using other Mesa gallium drivers for games.
